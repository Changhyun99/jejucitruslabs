INGREDIENTS

Natural Cosmetic Ingredients

Introducing our proprietary natural cosmetic ingredients upcycled from citrus peel.
Every ingredient begins from the nature of Jeju.

Citrus Peel Water (귤박수)

INCI ID: 41176

Citrus water extracted from citrus peel remaining after juicing through our proprietary process. With completed INCI international registration, it can be used as a global cosmetic ingredient. It is JEJUCITRUSLABS' signature ingredient with excellent skin soothing and moisturizing effects.

Citrus Unshiu Peel Extract

Citrus Unshiu Peel Extract

A flavonoid-rich ingredient extracted from Jeju citrus peel, functioning as an antioxidant and whitening cosmetic ingredient. Active compounds are maximally preserved through our green chemistry extraction process.

Carrot Leaf Extract

Daucus Carota Sativa Leaf Extract

Extracted from the leaves of Jeju-grown carrots, this ingredient is rich in vitamins and minerals, helping to nourish the skin and provide antioxidant benefits.

Cabbage Extract

Brassica Oleracea Capitata Extract

Containing Vitamin U and Vitamin K extracted from cabbage, this functional ingredient helps soothe and regenerate the skin.

Cabbage Leaf Water

Brassica Oleracea Capitata Leaf Water

A natural water distilled from cabbage leaves, effective for soothing sensitive skin and providing moisture — a naturally-derived ingredient.

Sargassum Horneri Extract

Sargassum Horneri Extract

A marine ingredient extracted from Sargassum Horneri harvested in Jeju's waters, rich in minerals and antioxidants that help protect the skin and improve elasticity.

Sea Lettuce Extract

Ulva Pertusa Extract

A marine ingredient extracted from Ulva Pertusa along Jeju's coast, with excellent moisturizing properties that help strengthen the skin barrier and brighten the complexion.
