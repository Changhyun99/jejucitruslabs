WHY WE EXIST

Left Behind. Loved Again.

Every year, over 50,000 tons of citrus pomace — the byproduct left after juicing — is discarded in Jeju. Citrus has been used as traditional medicine for centuries, and even the peel has long been brewed as tea. Yet once the juice is extracted, all that remaining value is simply thrown away.

JEJULABS was born from that sense of loss. We rediscover the essence hidden in what has been left behind, reinterpret its value, and bring it into people's everyday lives. That is why we exist.

Before asking what to make, we first ask why. We believe that when the reason is clear, the method and the result follow naturally.

KEY INGREDIENT

Gyulbaksu — Born from Discarded Peels

A natural ingredient derived from citrus pomace left after juicing Jeju's pesticide-free citrus, using only water and heat through our proprietary distillation and extraction process. It contains over 90% natural monoterpenes with high antioxidant activity, delivering a subtle citrus aroma without any artificial fragrances.

Citrus Unshiu Pomace Water

Registered in KCA (Korea Cosmetic Association) Ingredient Dictionary

INCI International Registration Complete (Monograph ID: 41176)

INCI REGISTERED · GLOBAL · 2025.12.11

Our distillation process extracts beneficial compounds from citrus pomace while separating volatile essential oils and organic acids that can burden the soil. The remaining pomace is left in an environmentally safe state, contributing to resource circulation.
